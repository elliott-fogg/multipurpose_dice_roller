Customisable Dice Roller

Create some custom dice rollers! You can specify a Multiplier of a flat Constant to add to the roll. Click on the roll buttons at the bottom to remove them. Once you're done, click "Create Rollers!" and the rolls will be created up here at the top. You can assign names to them, and the roll formula will show up when you hover over them. Once you've created your rolls, you can also bookmark the page to return to it, and pass the URL to other people.
